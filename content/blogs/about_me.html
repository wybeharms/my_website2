Introduction

Hi, so as you know by now my name is Wybe (although on my passport my first name is actually Christiaan, but that is a story for another time). I was born in Prague and then lived in Barcelona, Paris and Amsterdam with my family. After graduating from the international school of Amsterdam I moved back to Barcelona to study at ESADE Business School. In my fourth year I did my exchange in Austin, Texas at The University of Texas which was a great experience! Now I am very excited to continue my academics at LBS doing analytics!
